## Verdict request

Given the findings already drafted for {app_name}, produce the overall
verdict block that goes at the top of the report.

### Findings (already drafted, summary)

{findings_summary}

### Required output

* emoji: one of "🟢", "🟡", "🔴". Choose based on the finding mix:
    - 🔴 if any critical findings exist
    - 🟡 if no critical but at least one high
    - 🟢 if only medium/low (or no findings)
* summary: a single sentence, plain English, 8-14 words. Names what the
  customer should walk away knowing. Examples:
    - "Needs a few fixes before sharing publicly."
    - "Strong foundation, polish the listing pages and you're shipping."
    - "Two critical issues to clear before the launch link goes wider."
* narrative: 3-5 sentences. Frames the audit. Acknowledges what is
  already working (be specific, reference at least one concrete strength
  you saw in the evidence). Then names the top one or two issues to fix
  first, in plain language. Closes with a forward-looking sentence on
  pacing the rest.

### Voice rules

* No em-dashes. Use colons, periods, or two sentences instead.
* No "Certainly", "Overall", "In summary". Start with substance.
* Plain English. The customer reads this first; it sets the tone for the
  whole report.
* Do not repeat the same finding wording verbatim. Paraphrase.

Return the structured response only.
