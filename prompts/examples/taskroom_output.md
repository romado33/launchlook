# TaskRoom

## What it is

TaskRoom is a shared to-do list for small teams. You add tasks, assign them to teammates, and mark them done as the work gets finished. It replaces ad-hoc spreadsheets and Slack threads with a single place where everyone knows who's doing what.

## Who it's for

Small business owners and team leads who want to track work across 2–5 teammates without setting up a heavy tool like Asana or Jira.

## Get started

1. Sign up with your email and choose a password.
2. From the **Settings** tab, click **Invite Teammate** and add the email addresses of the people you work with.
3. Open the **Tasks** tab and click **Add Task** to create your first task.
4. Click **Assign** on the task to choose who should own it.
5. When the work is done, click **Mark Done** to clear it from the active list.

## What you can do

- Add a new task with a title, description, and due date.
- Assign any task to a teammate from your Team list.
- Mark tasks done as you finish them.
- Invite teammates from the **Settings** tab.
- Get an email when a teammate assigns you a task or marks one done.

## Common questions

**How do I invite teammates?**
Go to the **Settings** tab and click **Invite Teammate**. Add their email address and they'll get an invite link. They can join right away.

**How do I remove a teammate?**
From the **Team** tab, click the teammate's name and choose **Remove**. They lose access right away, but their past task history stays.

**Can I cancel my account?**
Yes — from **Settings**, scroll to the bottom and click **Delete Account**. Your data is removed within 30 days.
