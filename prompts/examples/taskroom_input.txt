APP NAME: TaskRoom

ONE-LINE DESCRIPTION:
A simple shared to-do list for small teams.

TARGET USER:
Small business owners or team leads who want to assign and track tasks without using something heavy like Asana or Jira.

MAIN WORKFLOW:
Create a task, assign it to a teammate, mark it done when finished.

PLATFORM BUILT WITH: Lovable
