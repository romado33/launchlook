The service

LaunchLook provides a founder-reviewed pre-launch checkup of your publicly accessible web application. We capture screenshots and HTML from your live URL, draft findings, and a founder personally reviews and curates every finding before delivery. We report issues visible to end users and supply paste-ready fix prompts for AI builders. We do not guarantee your app is secure, bug-free, or ready for production.

Not a security audit

Our checkup is not penetration testing or compliance certification. The Scale Up ($49) and Pro ($99) tiers include a basic cross-user visibility check, not a full security audit. The Pro Package integrations review covers configuration of Stripe, auth, email, and analytics services from the customer-facing surface only; it is not a security review of those providers. For security-focused reviews, use a dedicated security scanner or hire a security professional.
