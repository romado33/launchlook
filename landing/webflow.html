Scope

What we check on your Webflow site

  • 1 Form submissions actually work: the November 2024 Webflow change silently broke form handlers for hundreds of sites. Agencies lost months of leads. We test every form's submit path and flag the ones quietly dropping submissions.
  • 2 noindex / robots.txt not blocking your live URL: the “Disable Webflow subdomain indexing” toggle is a silent killer. We flag any page that's still set to noindex, and any robots.txt rule blocking your production host.
  • 3 JSON-LD / schema present and valid: per-page structured data is missed by every Webflow site we've reviewed. Only Framer plugins catch it. We do.
  • 4 Designer-to-live mismatches: placeholder “Lorem ipsum” or default field values still on the production page, broken component instances, hidden elements that show up published, interactions that worked in Designer preview but don't trigger live.
  • 5 Mobile breakpoint breakage at 991 / 767 / 478: Webflow's three native breakpoints. We render every page at each one and flag overflow, text overlap, broken nav, hidden CTAs.
  • 6 Trust pages and CMS data: missing /privacy or /terms, empty CMS collection states, fake-looking testimonials, dummy “John Doe” team cards still on the published page.

Questions

Webflow FAQ

Do you have access to my Webflow account?+

No. We only need your live URL (your .webflow.io subdomain or your custom domain). We don't request Workspace access, Editor seats, Designer logins, or your CMS API key. If a tool ever asks for those things just to spot broken UI, be wary.

Does this work for staging / preview URLs?+

Yes. Your .webflow.io preview URL works fine, as long as it's reachable. We do flag whether the preview is set to noindex (often a good thing for staging, sometimes accidentally still set when you cut over to custom domain).

Why is this so much cheaper than a Webflow Expert audit?+

A human-only audit on Codeable or Webflow Experts is $899–$4,800 because every minute is billable. We capture every page at every breakpoint, run the standard Webflow checks (forms, noindex, schema, breakpoints) automatically, and then a founder reads through every finding to drop the false positives and tighten the language before it ships. Sub-$100 price, founder-reviewed quality.

What if you flag something that isn't actually broken?+

That's what the founder review catches. Every finding is verified against the actual screenshots and live HTML before delivery. Anything that isn't grounded in real evidence gets dropped, not shipped. If a finding still doesn't match what you see, email us within 7 days for a full refund.

Will you catch the November 2024 form failure?+

Yes. We submit a probe payload to every form on your site and flag any that silently fail to send. The Nov 2024 Webflow update broke form handlers for many sites in a way that still shows a success message while dropping the submission server-side. That's the pattern most agencies don't catch for months.

I built my site with Webflow. Why do I still need a review?+

Webflow is great at building. It can’t walk your site as a confused first-time visitor, test whether your contact form actually sends, or catch the layout that collapses at the 767 breakpoint.

Those gaps only appear when a real person uses your site cold. LaunchLook is that person: someone who has never seen your site, trying to do the thing you built it for. Most sites have 5–10 things a first visitor would notice before you do — usually form failures, mobile breakage, and missing trust signals.

Does this replace the Webflow University pre-launch checklist?+

No, it complements it. The Webflow University checklist is the right place to start if you have 2 hours and know what to look for. LaunchLook is the second pair of eyes that catches what you stopped seeing. Many of our customers run the Webflow checklist first, then send us the URL to confirm.

Is this a security audit?+

No. We focus on what's visible: broken UI, broken forms, missing schema, accidentally indexable pages, mobile breakage. For deep security, you don't need much on a Webflow site (the platform handles most of it), but if you have custom code blocks or third-party embeds you want pen-tested, that's a different product.

Can I white-label the report for my client?+

On the Pro ($99) tier, yes. We deliver a white-label-friendly PDF with no LaunchLook branding in the body, plus personal Q&A (up to 5 questions answered within 24 hours) you can relay to your client or in-house team.
