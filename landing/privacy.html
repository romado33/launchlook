Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

LaunchLook ("we," "us") provides pre-launch checkup services for web applications. This policy describes what we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

  • Purchase info: name, email, and payment details (processed by Stripe — we do not store card numbers).
  • Intake form: your app URL, description, platform, and optional test account credentials for Launch Pack audits.
  • Audit artifacts: screenshots and notes from reviewing your public app.

How we use it

We use your information only to deliver your checkup report, send transactional email (welcome, delivery, follow-up), and improve our service. We do not sell your data.

Test account credentials

If you provide test logins for a Launch Pack audit, we use them only during the audit and delete them within 24 hours of delivery.

Retention

Audit screenshots and notes are kept for 30 days, then deleted. Your customer record in our internal workspace may be kept longer for support and referral tracking.

Third parties

  • Stripe (payments)
  • Resend or similar (transactional email)
  • Notion (internal report delivery and operations)
  • Tally.so or similar (intake forms)

Your rights

Email hello@launchlook.app to request access, correction, or deletion of your data.

This is a starter policy for an early-stage service. Have a lawyer review before scaling.
