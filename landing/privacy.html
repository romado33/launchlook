Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

LaunchLook ("we," "us") provides AI-drafted, founder-reviewed pre-launch checkup services for web applications. This policy describes what we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

  • Purchase info: name, email, and payment details (processed by Stripe; we do not store card numbers).
  • Intake form (Tally): your app URL, description, builder/platform (Lovable, Bolt, v0, Cursor, or similar), tier purchased, and optional temporary test account credentials for Scale Up ($49) and Pro ($99) audits.
  • Audit artifacts: screenshots and notes from reviewing your publicly reachable app. Your delivered report may include these screenshots.

What not to submit

Please use the intake form only (not email) for credentials. Do not send:

  • Your personal password or production admin credentials
  • API keys, Stripe keys, Supabase service keys, or database connection strings
  • Private customer data, health information, children's data, or financial records unless you have permission and understand the risks

For Scale Up ($49) and Pro ($99) user data isolation checks, create temporary test accounts only. Delete or rotate those credentials after we deliver your report.

How we use your information

We use your information only to deliver your checkup report, send transactional email, handle refunds, and improve our service. We do not sell your data.

AI processing

We use AI (Anthropic's Claude or OpenAI's GPT models) to analyze screenshots and HTML from your live URL. Your app data is sent to these providers only for the purpose of generating your audit. We do not use your data to train any models. Screenshots and HTML are not retained by the AI providers beyond the duration of the audit. The founder review happens locally on our systems. Your data never leaves what's needed for AI analysis and your founder-reviewed report.

Test account credentials

If you provide test logins for a Scale Up ($49) or Pro ($99) audit, we use them only during the audit. We will not make purchases, change production data, delete records, invite users, send messages, or perform destructive actions unless you explicitly agree in writing beforehand. Credentials are deleted within 24 hours of report delivery.

Retention

Audit artifacts (screenshots and review notes) are kept for 30 days, then deleted.

Customer record means: your name, email, order date, tier purchased, app URL, delivery status, and support/refund history. We may keep this longer in our internal workspace (Notion) for support, tax, refund handling, and referral tracking.

Third-party services

  • Stripe: payment processing
  • Tally: post-purchase intake forms
  • Notion: internal operations and customer tracking
  • Resend: report delivery email (with your Main Report and Quick Start Guide attached), plus other transactional email (welcome, follow-up)
  • Anthropic (Claude) and/or OpenAI (GPT): AI analysis of screenshots and HTML for the initial audit draft. Inputs are processed for the audit and not used for model training. See AI processing above.

Your rights

Email hello@launchlook.app to request access, correction, or deletion of your data.
