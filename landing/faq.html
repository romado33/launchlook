Common questions

Tiers, scope, data handling, refunds

What's the difference between Starter, Scale Up, and Pro?+

Starter ($19) covers the 10 things a first-time visitor would notice and need fixed before launch, ranked by impact. Best for: founders within a week of launching who want fast targeted fixes.

Scale Up ($49) is the full audit (up to 30 findings across every category), plus a user data isolation check using 2 test accounts to verify users can't see each other's data, plus a Quick Start Guide PDF you can give your users. Best for: founders 1–4 weeks out who want thorough coverage including the user data isolation check.

Pro ($99) is the deep audit (everything in Scale Up, plus up to 40 findings), a review of your operational integrations (Stripe, auth, email, analytics setup) to catch infrastructure gaps that aren't visible from the app itself, and a 30-minute Loom walkthrough where a founder walks you through the report and answers your questions. Best for: founders who want maximum confidence before paid traffic or investor demos.

Which platforms do you support?+

Any vibe-coded web app with a public URL. We often review Lovable, Bolt, Base44, and Replit apps. Also v0, Cursor-built apps, Webflow sites, and others. If your builder outputs a shareable link, we can usually review it.

Is this only for mobile apps?+

No. We review web apps built with any vibe-coding tool (Lovable, Bolt, v0, Cursor, etc.). “Phone check” means we load your URL at mobile width in the browser, not the App Store.

What do you need from me?+

Your public or staging URL, a one-line description, and your builder. For Scale Up Package ($49) and Pro Package ($99), two temporary test accounts so we can check data isolation. Pro Package also asks a couple of questions about your integrations (Stripe / auth / email / analytics) so we can review them.

Do you need access to my code or GitHub?+

No. We only need your public URL (and for Scale Up / Pro Package, two temporary test accounts). We never ask for GitHub access, your codebase, your builder login, or any access keys. If a tool ever asks for those things just to spot broken UI, be wary.

How is this different from a security scanner?+

We focus on what visitors see: broken UI, placeholders, trust pages. For deep security, use something like VAS. Use both if you can.

Lovable already has SEO review. Why this?+

SEO tools help Google. We help the friend you send the link to: dead buttons, dev tools on prod, missing legal pages, fake-looking reviews.

How are you different from PageLens AI?+

Both scan your live site and surface findings. We're built for vibe coders shipping with Lovable / Cursor / Bolt; PageLens is built for SEO-focused marketers. Full comparison →

Staging URLs OK?+

Yes if they're reachable on the internet (Lovable preview, Vercel staging, etc.). localhost won't work.

How do I know my data is safe?+

We only access your app through the public URL and any temporary test accounts you provide. We don't request code access, database access, or access keys. Credentials are submitted through the intake form only and deleted promptly after delivery. See our privacy policy.

Can I share my report publicly?+

Yes, opt-in. After delivery, every report has a private URL like launchlook.app/r/{your-slug}. By default it's private (only you can see it). Reply 'share' to your delivery email and we'll flip it public so you can post the link on Reddit, Twitter, your site footer, anywhere. Reply 'unshare' to flip it back.
