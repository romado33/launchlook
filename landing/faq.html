Frequently Asked Questions

Everything buyers usually ask before booking a checkup. If your question isn't here, email hello@launchlook.app and we'll answer (and probably add it to this page).

What do we mean by human-tested workflows and human review?+

Human-tested workflows. We do not only look at screenshots. A real person clicks through signup, booking, contact, and checkout paths, submits forms, and checks whether confirmation, thank-you, and notification emails actually arrive. That is how we catch “the form looked fine but nothing ever emailed anyone.”

Human-reviewed findings. AI helps surface issues quickly. Every finding still goes through a person before you see it: false positives dropped, unclear wording rewritten, and gaps added when something matters for your app.

You get a curated list from real walkthroughs, not a raw tool dump.

Which platforms do you support?+

Any vibe-coded web app with a public URL. We often review Lovable, Bolt, Base44, and Replit apps. Also v0, Cursor-built apps, Webflow sites, and others. If your builder outputs a shareable link, we can usually review it.

Is this only for mobile apps?+

No. We review web apps built with any vibe-coding tool (Lovable, Bolt, v0, Cursor, etc.). “Phone check” means we load your URL at mobile width in the browser, not the App Store.

Do you need my code, GitHub, or any account access?+

No. We only need your public URL (and for Scale Up / Pro Package, two temporary test accounts to check data isolation). We never ask for GitHub access, your codebase, your builder login, or any access keys. If a tool ever asks for those things just to spot broken UI, be wary.

We don’t request database access or store credentials. Anything you provide through the intake form is deleted promptly after delivery. See our privacy policy.

I’m worried you’ll steal my idea or pass it off as your own. How do I know you won’t?+

That’s a fair thing to worry about, and I want to be direct about it.

We only see what anyone on the internet can see. We visit your live public URL — the same thing a first-time visitor would see. We never ask for your source code, your Lovable / Cursor / Webflow account, your GitHub, or any internal data. If your unreleased features aren’t publicly visible yet, we can’t see them either.

One person reviews your audit: me (Rob), the founder. Your URL and findings aren’t passed to a team, outsourced, or stored in a shared system. Screenshots are kept only until the report is delivered, then deleted.

I’m a technical writer, not a developer or a competitor. I’m not in a position to rebuild what you’ve built, and I have no interest in doing so. My reputation depends entirely on being trustworthy — one bad experience goes public immediately in the vibe-coder community.

If you still want something in writing before sharing your URL, email hello@launchlook.app and I’ll send a one-page mutual NDA by return. No questions asked.

How is this different from a security scanner?+

We focus on what visitors see: broken UI, placeholders, trust pages. For deep security, use something like VAS. Use both if you can.

I built my app with Lovable / Cursor / Bolt. Why do I still need a review?+

Your AI builder was optimized to generate code quickly. It wasn’t designed to walk your app as a confused first-time visitor, test whether your confirmation emails arrive, or notice the button that stops working at mobile width.

Those gaps only appear when a real person uses your app cold. LaunchLook is that person: someone who has never seen your app, trying to do the thing you built it for. Most vibe-coded apps have 5–15 things a first user would notice before you do.

Lovable’s built-in SEO check and similar tools help Google find your page. We help the friend you just sent the link to: the dead button, the form that silently fails, the mobile layout that breaks, the “Dev Bypass” sign-in still showing in production.

My app is still on a preview URL. Can you review it now?+

Yes. Lovable preview URLs, Vercel staging URLs, and any publicly reachable URL work. We flag anything that will need to be different on your custom domain (like a missing canonical or noindex setting). localhost won’t work.

Can I share my report publicly?+

Yes, opt-in. After delivery, every report has a private URL like launchlook.app/r/{your-slug}. By default it's private (only you can see it). Reply 'share' to your delivery email and we'll flip it public so you can post the link on Reddit, Twitter, your site footer, anywhere. Reply 'unshare' to flip it back.

How is a Webflow checkup different from a vibe-coded app checkup?+

Webflow is a site builder, not an AI app generator. The same human review applies, but what we look for shifts to Webflow-specific launch risks: form submissions that silently fail, accidental noindex, missing JSON-LD, Designer-to-live mismatches, and breakage at Webflow’s 991 / 767 / 478 breakpoints.

Fix prompts are written in Webflow Designer language (Pages, elements, settings), not “paste this into Lovable.” We still only need your live URL, never your Webflow account or Designer login. Scale Up and Pro tiers that include a user data isolation check are aimed at vibe-coded apps with logins; most marketing Webflow sites don’t need test accounts.
