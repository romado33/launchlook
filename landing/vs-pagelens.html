Honest trade-offs

When PageLens is the better choice

If you need ongoing weekly SEO monitoring, a marketing-team dashboard, or an instant scan with no waiting, PageLens is purpose-built for that workflow and beats LaunchLook on those axes. We take a few days because a real person tests your workflows (forms, emails that should follow) and reviews every finding before it ships; if you need an answer in the next 60 seconds, PageLens is faster. We don't think one tool fits everyone, and we'd rather you pick the one that fits the job.
