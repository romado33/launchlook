What AI builders can’t test

Five things your builder can’t catch

These gaps only appear when a real person uses your app cold. They happen on almost every AI-built app we review.

1. Silent form failures

Your signup or contact form looks fine. Click submit. Nothing arrives. No confirmation email, no error. Your builder generated the UI; it never tested the delivery. This is the most common launch blocker we find.

2. Mobile layout problems

AI builders preview on desktop. The button that can’t be tapped on a phone, the text that overflows off the right edge, the navigation that hides the main CTA — these are invisible until a real device opens the link.

3. Dev artifacts in production

“Dev Bypass” sign-in buttons. “Seed sample data.” Placeholder team photos. A zero-review rating showing “4.5 (0)”. These erode trust instantly — and AI builders ship them by default.

4. Missing trust pages

No privacy policy. No terms. A contact email that goes nowhere. Dead footer links. These are the first things a skeptical visitor checks — and the most common reason they leave without buying.

5. Copy that reads AI-written

Tricolons (“comprehensive, elegant, and powerful”). Filler phrases (“it’s important to note that”). Generic hero claims with no specifics. Visitors can’t always name it, but they feel it and leave.

Yes. Lovable preview URLs, Vercel staging URLs, and any publicly reachable URL work. We flag anything that will need to be different on your custom domain (like a missing canonical or noindex setting).
