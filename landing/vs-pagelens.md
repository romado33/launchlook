# LaunchLook vs PageLens AI

Neutral comparison for buyers choosing a pre-launch audit tool.

## LaunchLook

- **Audience:** Vibe coders shipping with Lovable, Bolt, Cursor, v0, Replit, Base44, Webflow.
- **Job:** One-off pre-launch checkup before sharing the link.
- **Deliverable:** Human-reviewed findings, paste-ready fix prompts for your AI builder, PDFs on paid tiers.
- **Wedge:** Founder curates every finding; plain English; “what would a first visitor notice?”

## PageLens AI

- **Audience:** SEO-focused marketers and operators.
- **Job:** Recurring site scans, SEO-oriented findings, dashboards.
- **Wedge:** Fast automated scans, SEO workflow.

## Overlap

Both take a URL and return a list of issues on the live site.

## Where they differ

| | LaunchLook | PageLens |
|---|------------|----------|
| Primary buyer | Founder shipping an AI-built app | Marketer optimizing search |
| Workflow testing | Real submits + email checks by a person | Page scan only |
| Finding review | Human reviews every finding | Tool-first |
| Fix format | Paste-ready builder prompts | Varies |
| Pricing entry | Free 2 findings, then $19+ | Lower entry scan pricing |

## When to use which

- **LaunchLook** if you are about to post your app link and want a stranger’s-eye check with fixes you can paste into Lovable/Cursor.
- **PageLens** if you want ongoing SEO monitoring and marketer-oriented reports.
