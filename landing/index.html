Examples of what we catch

Illustrative, not exhaustive — these show up constantly on AI-built apps:

  • Dead buttons and broken links
  • Layout breaks at mobile width
  • Placeholder or generic AI copy still on the page
  • Missing privacy or terms pages
  • Signup or contact forms that fail silently
  • Confirmation or thank-you emails that never arrive
  • Test data or dev shortcuts visible in production
  • Login or checkout pages that show “Not Secure” in the browser

The human difference

  • Tested, not just scanned. Desktop and mobile width in the browser, plus real form submits and inbox checks.
  • Reviewed, not dumped. False positives cut before delivery.
  • Builder prompts. Paste into Lovable, Bolt, Cursor, v0, and more.
