Real things we've caught on AI-built apps

Any of these sound familiar?

  • ✓noindex still on after publishing — your app is invisible to Google because the “block search engines” toggle was never turned off.
  • ✓Contact form looks fine, silently drops submissions — the form works in the builder preview but on the live site nothing arrives.
  • ✓Test Stripe key still in production — charges work in your testing but real customers can’t pay.
  • ✓Login works on desktop, breaks at 375px — the submit button is hidden off-screen on every phone.
  • ✓Confirmation email never arrives — users sign up, see nothing, assume it’s broken, and don’t come back.
  • ✓Placeholder copy from the AI still on the page — “Lorem ipsum” or generated filler text visible to real visitors.
