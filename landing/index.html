What we catch

  • ✓ Dead buttons and broken links on your live site.
  • ✓ Layout breaks at phone width (text overlap, hidden CTAs, broken nav).
  • ✓ Leftover placeholders: "Your Company Name," lorem ipsum, default Lovable/Bolt copy.
  • ✓ Missing privacy and terms pages, plus other trust gaps a stranger would clock instantly.
  • ✓ Fake-looking testimonials and default "John Doe" team cards still on the live page.
  • ✓ Dev tools and test data accidentally visible on production.
  • ✓ Generic meta and social previews that look unfinished when someone shares your link.
  • ✓ Cross-user data leaks where signed-in users can see each other's data (Scale Up and Pro, with 2 test accounts).
  • ✓ Obvious visible risks like exposed access keys or public admin routes (Scale Up and Pro).
  • ✓ Integration misconfig across Stripe, login, email, and analytics setup (Pro only).

Test logins are used only during the review and deleted within 24 hours of delivery. This is a launch-readiness checkup, not a full security audit.

Different by design

Why founders pick LaunchLook over auto-scanners

Auto-scanners fire 100+ checks at your app and dump everything they found into a dashboard. We do something smaller on purpose.

  • Founder-curated, personally reviewed

    Every finding gets read by Rob before delivery. False positives get dropped, wording that doesn't land gets cut, and only the things you should actually fix make it into the final PDF. Auto-scanners ship the raw dump, false positives included.

  • Plain-English findings, not cryptic codes

    Every finding reads like a friend wrote it. The 10 to 40 things that actually matter, ranked by impact, written so a non-technical founder can act on them. Elsewhere: pages of severity codes you'd have to Google one by one.

  • Paste-ready fix prompts for your builder

    Each finding ships with a copy-paste prompt written for Lovable, Bolt, v0, Cursor, or whichever AI builder you used. Paste it in, get the fix back. Elsewhere: technical write-ups your AI builder can't act on directly.

Want a deep technical security scan too? Run an auto-scanner alongside ours. They catch different things. We're the human pass that tells you what your first 10 users will actually notice.
