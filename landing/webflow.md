# LaunchLook for Webflow

> Human-reviewed pre-launch checkup before forms silently break, noindex blocks Google, or your client finds the typo.

## Who this is for

Webflow freelancers, agencies, and in-house teams before client handoff. **URL only** — no Workspace or Designer login required.

## What we check

- Form submissions (including post–Nov 2024 failure modes)
- Accidental noindex and robots.txt blocking production
- JSON-LD / structured data
- Designer-to-live mismatches
- Mobile breakage at Webflow breakpoints: **991px, 767px, 478px**

A human submits forms where possible, checks notification emails, and reviews every finding before delivery. Fix prompts are written in **Webflow Designer language** (Pages, Publish, etc.), not “paste into Lovable.”

## Pricing (USD)

Same ladder as the main site: Free (2 findings), Starter $19, Scale Up $49, Pro $99.

## How it differs from vibe-coded audits

Vibe-coded apps: broken flows, placeholder UI, dev tools on prod, user data isolation on higher tiers.

Webflow sites: builder-specific launch risks above; most marketing sites do not need login test accounts.
