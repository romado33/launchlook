# LaunchLook — Home

> Human-reviewed pre-launch checkup for vibe-coded web apps and Webflow. One last look before you launch.

## Value proposition

Find embarrassing bugs and confusing screens before your first users do. Send your live URL. LaunchLook loads it at desktop and mobile width in the browser, runs the workflows that matter (signup, forms, checkout), and checks whether confirmation and thank-you emails actually arrive. A founder reviews every finding before you see it. You get screenshots, ranked fixes, and paste-ready prompts for your builder.

**No repo access needed.** Public or staging URL is enough.

## Platforms

Lovable, Bolt, Base44, Replit, v0, Cursor, Webflow, and similar platforms.

## How it works

1. **Submit URL** — Free audit on the homepage, or pay then complete intake.
2. **Test and review** — Desktop and mobile width passes, real workflow tests, then curated findings.
3. **Delivery** — Email with findings and fix prompts (PDFs on paid tiers).
